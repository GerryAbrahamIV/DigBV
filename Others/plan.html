Mission

The website will be made in order to increase the popularity of Digger's Volleyball, and make visitors of the website to be interested to go to Digger's Volleyball. In the end, the mission is to increase the amount of people entering and playing at Digger's Volleyball, especially those in high school and young adults. Indirectly increasing the visitor, income and sales as well as popularity of the place.

Success Evaluation

The increment of visitors, especially ones in high school and young adults, as well as the increment of income and popularity.

Target Audience

High schoolers and young adults, although older people can be potential audience as well.

The site will be made in a more simple design, focusing on the *fun* part of volleyball. The colors should be more vibrant yet not too colorful and flashy.
