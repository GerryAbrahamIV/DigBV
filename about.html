About us

Opening in 2007 in Marakson, Diggers Beach Volleyball is a place where visitors can have fun playing beach volleyball.

Beach volleyball is a great example of a family sport, as it is safe and simple, and not too tiring. We guarantee fun times playing at Diggers, and hoping to not just help you in getting a fun experience, but also increase the community of volleyball players.

We hold competitive volleyball tourneys and social volleyball plays, and we also have a licensed bar that have happy hour every 6-7pm. We also believe that this sport must be spread to be known by the youths as well, so we are hoping you all to be here with us!
